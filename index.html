About me

I am a researcher in experimental nuclear physics, focusing on nuclear reactions, nuclear data evaluation, and their applications in astrophysics, space, and medical physics.

My research includes R-matrix analysis, Monte Carlo simulations using Geant4, and experimental studies of nuclear reactions at astrophysical energies.

I have worked at NCSR Demokritos, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Culham Centre for Fusion Energy, LAPP (France), and CERN (ISOLDE and n_TOF collaboration), and I currently collaborate with the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA).
