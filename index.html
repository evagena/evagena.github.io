Bio

I hold a PhD in Nuclear Physics from the Department of Physics at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. My research focuses on the experimental and computational study of nuclear reactions, nuclear data evaluation, and applications of nuclear physics in astrophysics, space science, and medical physics.

I have worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Nuclear and Particle Physics of NCSR Demokritos and at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, and I have collaborated with international research centers including Culham Centre for Fusion Energy (UK), LAPP in France, and CERN through ISOLDE and the n_TOF collaboration. I have also contributed to nuclear data evaluation activities of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), where I currently serve as a research associate.

My research includes nuclear reaction experiments at astrophysical energies, analysis and evaluation of cross sections, R-matrix calculations, and Monte Carlo simulations using Geant4 for applications in radiation detectors, dosimetry, space environments, and neutron experiments.
